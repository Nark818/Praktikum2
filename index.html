    About

    DanTDM, whose real name is Daniel Robert Middleton, is a popular British YouTuber, professional gamer, and internet personality, best known for his Minecraft-related content. He was born on November 8, 1991, in Aldershot, England. DanTDM (short for The Diamond Minecart) gained widespread recognition for his family-friendly videos, mainly focused on Minecraft, but he has also covered a variety of other games like Roblox, Fortnite, and Pokémon.
